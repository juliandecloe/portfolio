Buiten Beeld - Passieproject

This schoolproject I didn't do alone. I did this with one other student. The passion project was a project where we had the opportunity to create our own project. No guidelines, no nothing. My teammate Gwen and I wanted to do something for children. It was summer, so we chose to design an app that motivates children to play outside. Children nowadays like phones as much as playing outside, so why not combine them?

That is why we came up with 'Buiten Beeld'. The name literally means 'Outside of View' and 'Besides screen'. This app contains multiple games for the children to enjoy themselves outside.

WorldTravel - Project Web

For this schoolproject I had to design a traveling app featuring excisting Web API's (application programming interface). So I chose to design an app called 'WorldTravel'. This app gives you the opportunity to look for thousands of cities and information about these cities. Next to that you can put cities in favourites to check them out later and you can even make multiple schedules for holidays.

The Web API's I chose are:

Discord Remake - Frontend Development

This schoolproject is a very free project. The name of the subject says it already: Frontend Development. We were allowed to choose a website ourselves which we then had to rebuild. We did have to choose between making the website fully responsive (usable for every screensize) or work on the surface plane (making the website more attractive and interactive). I chose for the surface plane, because even though I can make a website fully responsive, I had more fun working on the surface plane because then I could use more javascript, which I really enjoy doing.

I chose the Discord website, because it is a really impressive website with a lot of features.

The Big Escape - Inleiding Programmeren

This was the first time I used Javascript. This project was a project focussed on javascript. Most people made something easy like a tamagotchi, but I wanted bigger and better. That is why I made a small escape room called 'The Big Escape'.

The Big Escape is sadly not responsive which means you are forced to play it in a specific browser window size. Clicking on the following button will automatically open a new window in the right size, so don't change the window size!

Koffieapparaat - Ucer Centered Design

Here I had to do research about users of coffee machines on the Hogeschool van Amsterdam (HvA). I started with research using interviews, observations, user experiences, overfiew maps and desk research. After that I made a list of all the problems the coffee machines had and how that conflicts with the users goal.

From there on I started working on solutions for these problems and make them into a new interface. With this project I hope to show you how organised and clear I can work.

VRcafe Haarlem app

VRcafe Haarlem is a café where you can play VR games with friends and have a drink. It is also my sidejob. My boss asked me if I was interested in helping them design an app for future plans. I saw it as a good opportunity as experience and it seemed fun, so I said yes.

The app has a check-in function which decides how much you pay. The longer you are checked in, the more you pay. When you are checked in you can also gain experience and get achievements. Achievements contain small prices which you can collect in the café.

Due to Covid-19 the app sadly never actually launched and the app was cancelled, but the designs are still intact.
